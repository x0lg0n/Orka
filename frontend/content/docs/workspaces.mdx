A workspace is your agency's home on ORKA. It contains all your projects, team members, and billing settings in one place.

## Creating a Workspace

1. Click **Create Workspace** from the dashboard
2. Enter a name for your workspace
3. Set your default currency and timezone
4. Invite team members by email

## Workspace Roles

ORKA supports three roles within a workspace:

- **Owner**: Full access to all settings, billing, and team management
- **Admin**: Can manage projects, clients, and team members
- **Member**: Can work on assigned projects and view analytics

## Team Management

Invite team members by email from the workspace settings. Each member gets access based on their role.

## Workspace Settings

Configure your workspace defaults:

- **Currency**: Default currency for new projects
- **Timezone**: Used for deadline calculations
- **Branding**: Customize client-facing documents
- **Notifications**: Set up email and in-app notification preferences

<Callout type="info">
Each workspace is independent. If you work with multiple agencies, you can create separate workspaces for each.
</Callout>

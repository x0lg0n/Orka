## General

### What is ORKA?

ORKA is the financial operating system for service work. It handles proposals, escrow, verifying delivery, payouts, invoices, and records — so agencies and freelancers can focus on the actual work.

### Do I need a crypto wallet?

No. Managed mode uses email or Google login. Stellar runs silently underneath. Crypto-native users can optionally connect Freighter.

### Who pays the network fees?

ORKA does. Every transaction is sponsored, so it costs you nothing extra.

### Can ORKA run off with my money?

No. Releasing escrow requires both the client and ORKA to approve. A single leaked key can never drain your funds.

## Payments

### What currencies can I use?

Clients can pay in normal currency by card or bank; ORKA handles the conversion to USDC and payout back to fiat.

### How long do payouts take?

Bank transfers typically take 1-3 business days. Crypto payouts are instant once confirmed on Stellar.

### Are there any fees?

ORKA charges a small percentage on successful milestone releases. There are no setup fees, monthly fees, or hidden charges.

## Technical

### What blockchain does ORKA use?

ORKA uses the Stellar blockchain. It's fast, low-cost, and energy-efficient compared to other blockchains.

### What if I lose access to my account?

In managed mode, reset your password to regain access. In self-custody mode, your seed phrase is yours to protect.

### Is my data secure?

Yes. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We use AES-256 encryption and TLS 1.3 for all communications.

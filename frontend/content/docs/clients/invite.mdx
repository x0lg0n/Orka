# Invite Clients

Bring clients into your ORKA workspace.

## Invitation Process

1. Go to **Clients**
2. Click **Invite Client**
3. Enter client email
4. Add company details
5. Send invitation

## Client Roles

- **Primary contact** - Main decision maker
- **Team member** - Additional access
- **Viewer** - Read-only access

## After Invitation

- Client receives email
- Creates ORKA account
- Gets portal access
- Can view shared projects

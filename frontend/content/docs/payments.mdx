Payments in ORKA are tied to milestone releases. When a milestone is approved, funds are automatically released to the freelancer.

## Payment Flow

1. Client funds escrow
2. Freelancer delivers work
3. Client approves the milestone
4. ORKA verifies the release conditions
5. Funds are released to the freelancer
6. Invoice is auto-generated

## Payment Methods

Freelancers can receive payments via:

- **Bank Transfer**: Direct to your bank account
- **Crypto**: USDC to your Stellar wallet
- **Both**: Split between fiat and crypto

## Transaction History

View all payments in the project's payment history:

- Amount and currency
- Date and time
- Status (pending, processing, completed)
- Transaction hash on Stellar

<Callout type="tip">
All payment records are stored permanently on the Stellar blockchain. You can verify any transaction independently.
</Callout>

## Automatic Invoices

When a milestone is released, ORKA automatically:

1. Generates an invoice with project details
2. Emails it to both parties
3. Stores it in the project records
4. Includes it in your tax reports

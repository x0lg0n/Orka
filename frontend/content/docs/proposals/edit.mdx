# Edit Proposals

Fine-tune your proposals to match your needs.

## Editing Tools

- **Rich text editor** - Format text easily
- **Drag-and-drop** - Rearrange sections
- **Media support** - Add images and videos
- **Custom fields** - Add specific details

## Sections to Include

1. **Cover letter** - Personal introduction
2. **Scope of work** - Project details
3. **Timeline** - Delivery schedule
4. **Pricing** - Cost breakdown
5. **Terms** - Payment and legal terms

## Saving as Template

Save successful proposals as templates for future use.

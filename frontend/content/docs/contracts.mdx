Contracts formalize the agreement between you and your client. ORKA generates contracts automatically and manages digital signatures.

## Generating a Contract

1. After a proposal is accepted, ORKA auto-generates a contract
2. Review the terms and conditions
3. Send for signatures

## AI Contract Generation

ORKA's AI creates contracts based on:

- Project scope and deliverables
- Milestone pricing and payment terms
- Timeline and deadlines
- Standard legal protections

<Callout type="info">
ORKA contracts are legally binding digital agreements. Both parties must sign before work can begin.
</Callout>

## Digital Signatures

Both parties sign contracts digitally through ORKA:

1. Review the contract terms
2. Click **Sign** to apply your digital signature
3. The signed contract is stored in the project

## Version Management

When contract terms change:

1. ORKA creates a new version
2. Both parties are notified of changes
3. Previous versions are archived
4. Only the latest signed version is active

# Payment Tracking

Keep track of all invoice payments.

## Payment Methods

- Stellar wallet transfer
- Bank transfer
- Credit card
- Other methods

## Tracking Process

1. Client makes payment
2. ORKA detects transaction
3. Invoice marked as paid
4. Record updated automatically
5. Receipt generated

## Reconciliation

- Match payments to invoices
- Record partial payments
- Track outstanding balances
- Generate accounting reports

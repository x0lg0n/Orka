# Wallet FAQ

Common wallet-related questions.

## What is Freighter?

Freighter is a Stellar wallet browser extension that manages your XLM and Stellar assets.

## Is Freighter safe?

Yes, Freighter is open-source and widely used in the Stellar community. Private keys never leave the extension.

## Can I use multiple wallets?

Yes, Freighter supports multiple accounts. You can connect different wallets to ORKA.

## What if I lose access?

Use your recovery phrase to restore access. Keep this phrase secure and offline.

## How do I update Freighter?

Updates are automatic through your browser's extension store.

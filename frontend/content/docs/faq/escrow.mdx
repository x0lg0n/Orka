# Escrow FAQ

Common questions about ORKA escrow.

## How does escrow work?

Funds are held on Stellar blockchain until milestones are approved. This protects both clients and freelancers.

## What fees are charged?

ORKA charges minimal fees for escrow services. View our pricing page for details.

## What if there's a dispute?

ORKA provides mediation services. Both parties can present their case, and our team helps resolve issues.

## How long does release take?

Payments are released instantly upon milestone approval. Funds appear in your wallet within seconds.

## Can I get a refund?

Refunds are possible through mutual agreement or dispute resolution.

# Payments FAQ

Common payment questions.

## What currencies are supported?

ORKA supports XLM and multiple fiat currencies through Stellar anchors.

## How fast are payments?

Stellar transactions confirm in 3-5 seconds.

## What are the fees?

Stellar network fees are minimal (fractions of a cent). ORKA fees are listed on our pricing page.

## Can I reverse a payment?

Payments on Stellar are irreversible. Ensure all details are correct before sending.

## How do I check my balance?

View your balance in the Freighter extension or ORKA dashboard.

# Signatures

Digital signatures streamline the approval process.

## Signature Process

1. Send contract to client
2. Client reviews contract
3. Client adds digital signature
4. Both parties receive copies
5. Contract is marked as executed

## Legal Validity

- Compliant with e-signature laws
- Timestamped signatures
- Audit trail maintained
- Tamper-proof records

## Managing Signatures

- Track signature status
- Send reminders
- View signature history
- Download signed copies

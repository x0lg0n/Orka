Clients are the companies or individuals you work with. ORKA makes it easy to manage client relationships and give them visibility into projects.

## Adding Clients

1. Go to your workspace's **Clients** section
2. Click **Add Client**
3. Enter their name and email
4. Send an invitation

## Client Portal

Each client gets a read-only portal where they can:

- View project progress and milestones
- Approve or reject deliverables
- Fund escrow
- Track payments and invoices

<Callout type="tip">
Clients don't need an ORKA account to use the portal. They access it via a secure link sent to their email.
</Callout>

## Client Management

Manage all your clients from the workspace:

- **View all clients** and their associated projects
- **Track client activity** across all projects
- **Manage permissions** and access levels
- **Archive inactive clients** to keep your workspace clean

## Client Communication

ORKA keeps all project communication in one place:

- Proposal discussions
- Contract negotiations
- Milestone feedback
- Payment notifications

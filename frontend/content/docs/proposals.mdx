Proposals let you scope work, present pricing, and get client approval before starting a project.

## Creating a Proposal

1. Go to your project and click **New Proposal**
2. Use the AI generator to draft from a brief
3. Edit milestones, pricing, and terms
4. Send to the client for review

## AI Proposal Generator

ORKA's AI can generate a full proposal from a brief description. Just paste the client's requirements and the AI will create:

- Project scope and deliverables
- Milestone breakdown with pricing
- Timeline estimates
- Terms and conditions

<Callout type="tip">
The AI generator works best when you provide detailed client requirements. The more context you give, the better the proposal.
</Callout>

## Tracking Proposals

Track proposal status in real-time:

- **Draft**: Not yet sent
- **Sent**: Waiting for client review
- **Viewed**: Client has opened the proposal
- **Accepted**: Client approved the proposal
- **Declined**: Client declined the proposal

## Accepting Proposals

When a client accepts a proposal, ORKA automatically:

1. Creates the project with the proposed milestones
2. Generates a contract for both parties to sign
3. Prepares the escrow for funding

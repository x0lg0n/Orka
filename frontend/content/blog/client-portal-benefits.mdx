A client portal is more than a file-sharing hub. It's the central workspace where proposals become contracts, milestones get approved, and payments happen — all without leaving the browser.

Agencies that provide client portals report higher satisfaction, faster payment cycles, and fewer "where do I find this?" emails.

This guide — coming soon — covers why every agency needs a client portal and how to set one up.

## Why Every Agency Needs a Client Portal in 2025

- Clients can view proposals, sign contracts, and approve milestones in one place
- No more searching email threads for attachments
- Payment status is always visible — no "did you send the invoice?" questions
- Professional presentation that differentiates you from competitors

## What to Look for in a Client Portal

- Proposal to payment in one flow
- Milestone tracking and approval
- Escrow and payment integration
- Activity history and audit trail
- White-label or branded experience

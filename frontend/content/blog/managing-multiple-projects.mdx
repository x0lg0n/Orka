## Managing Multiple Projects Without Losing Your Mind

- Centralize client communication — no more context switching between email, Slack, and DMs
- Use milestone-based planning so every project has clear checkpoints
- Time-block your week by project type, not client urgency

## Why Scattered Projects Fail

When each project lives in its own silo, nothing is visible until something breaks. A unified view of all active projects helps you allocate time, spot bottlenecks, and deliver on time.

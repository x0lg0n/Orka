## 5 Pricing Strategies for Agencies

1. **Value-based pricing** — Price based on the outcome, not the effort
2. **Retainer models** — Predictable recurring revenue from ongoing engagements
3. **Milestone-based pricing** — Project broken into funded stages
4. **Tiered packages** — Good / Better / Best options for different budgets
5. **Performance-based** — Base + bonus tied to client outcomes

## Why Hourly Billing Hurts Your Agency

Hourly billing caps your income at your available hours. It disincentivizes efficiency and commoditizes your expertise. The most profitable agencies have moved away from hourly billing entirely.

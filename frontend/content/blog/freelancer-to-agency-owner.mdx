Going from solo freelancer to agency owner is the most challenging — and rewarding — transition in a service business career.

You go from doing the work to selling the work, managing the work, and building a team. It's a completely different skill set.

This guide — coming soon — covers the operational, financial, and mindset shifts that make the transition successful.

## The Freelancer to Agency Owner Transition

### Operations

What worked for a solo operator breaks at 2–3 team members. You need repeatable processes, clear handoffs, and a system that doesn't rely on you being in every conversation.

### Pricing

Agency pricing is different from freelance rates. You now carry team costs, tooling, overhead, and profit margin. Full guide covers how to price for agency profitability.

### Client Management

As a freelancer, you could keep everything in your head. As an agency owner, you need a system. CRM, project management, contract templates, payment processing — they all need to work together.

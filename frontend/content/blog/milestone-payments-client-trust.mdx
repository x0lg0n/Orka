Trust is the currency of client relationships. Nothing builds trust faster than a clear, predictable payment structure.

Milestone payments break a project into stages, each with its own deliverable and payment. The client sees progress. You get paid as you go. Both sides stay aligned.

## Why Milestone Payments Build Client Trust

- Clients see progress before releasing the next payment
- Smaller commitments feel safer than one large deposit
- Approval gates create natural review points
- Disputes are contained to individual milestones, not the whole project

## How to Structure Milestone Payments

Effective milestones are:

1. **Measurable** — Clear deliverable or completion criterion
2. **Proportional** — Payment matches the effort in that phase
3. **Sequential** — Each milestone builds on the previous one
4. **Reversible** — Poor work can be rejected before the next milestone
